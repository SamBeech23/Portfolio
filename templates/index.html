About me

I'm a final-year PhD researcher at The University of Bristol. I explore and identify human-based approaches to network optimisation, investigating the potential for visuomotor adaptation as a mechanism to alleviate latency-induced performance impairments in interactive technologies. I am also a teaching assistant at The University of Bath, where I teach statistics, databases, and machine learning.

I also work as a Digital Marketing Executive at The Ginger Agency. I currently manage a small team, where I oversee social media growth across Instagram, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, and YouTube for Vox Amplification, Korg, and Takamine Guitars.
